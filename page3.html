Approach

We approached this goal by collaborating with a dedicated team in a fast-paced environment, strategically implementing the Microsoft Bot Framework SDK, and leveraging LUIS services to improve the context capability of our banking bots.

Methodology 

The methodology involved a multidisciplinary team working in a fast-paced environment to develop and integrate Conversational AI and Digital Video Banking solutions. We meticulously utilized the Microsoft Bot Framework SDK and harnessed LUIS services to empower our banking bots with advanced context capabilities.
3

Insights

Through our efforts, we gained valuable insights into the power of technology in transforming customer engagement and boosting sales. Our experience highlighted the significance of strategic collaboration and the potential of AI-driven solutions in the financial sector.
4

Impact 

Our strategic utilization of the Microsoft Bot Framework SDK and LUIS services resulted in a remarkable 30% increase in customer interactions and sales, demonstrating the transformative impact of Conversational AI and Digital Video Banking in serving our American clients. Additionally, the improved context capabilities of our banking bots contributed to a 15% enhancement in customer engagement, underlining the effectiveness of our approach.
